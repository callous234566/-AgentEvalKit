# Target Customer Notes

## Segment 1: Remote Operator

Remote operators are project managers, analysts, and customer success leads who spend most of the day in meetings. They want healthier routines but feel schedule-constrained.

Pain points:

- Long meeting blocks leave little time for exercise.
- They feel low energy in the afternoon.
- They worry that exercise equipment will be noisy or distracting.

Likely hooks:

- "Walk through your 2 PM meeting without leaving your desk."
- "Make movement part of the calendar you already have."
- "Quiet enough for calls, small enough for apartments."

## Segment 2: Apartment Minimalist

Apartment minimalists care about space and visual clutter. They reject bulky home gym equipment.

Pain points:

- Limited storage.
- Shared walls and downstairs neighbors.
- Equipment that looks ugly in a living room.

Likely hooks:

- "A walking routine that disappears behind the sofa."
- "Home-office movement without building a home gym."

## Segment 3: Habit Rebuilder

Habit rebuilders have tried fitness plans before but quit because the plan required too much willpower. They respond to tiny habit framing.

Pain points:

- Overly ambitious fitness goals feel intimidating.
- They want visible progress without a complex routine.
- They respond to simple starter plans.

Likely hooks:

- "Start with ten minutes during email."
- "Pair walking with the work you already do."
