Your calendar is full, but your body is stuck in the same chair. Most fitness routines ask you to find more time. StrideLite helps you use the work time you already have.

- Focus speed presets for email, calls, and reading

## Objection Section

Will it be noisy? StrideLite is designed for low-speed walking during calls and focus blocks.

Will it fit? It folds to 5 inches high and stores vertically.

Will I keep using it? The included guide starts with 10-minute workday walking blocks.

Avoid guaranteed health or weight-loss claims. Keep the claim focused on routine, movement, and convenience.
