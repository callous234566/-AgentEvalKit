# Offer Brief: StrideLite Desk Treadmill

## Product

StrideLite is a compact under-desk walking treadmill for remote workers who want more movement during deep work. It is designed for apartments, home offices, and shared workspaces.

## Core Promise

Help desk-based professionals add low-intensity movement to the workday without changing their schedule or leaving their workspace.

## Audience

- Remote employees who sit for 6+ hours per day
- Freelancers and founders who work from home
- Apartment dwellers who need quiet equipment
- People who dislike traditional workouts but want a practical habit

## Differentiators

- Quiet motor rated for calls and shared spaces
- Folds vertically behind a door or sofa
- Simple speed presets for focus blocks
- No subscription required
- Includes a 30-day habit guide

## Main Objections

- "Will it be too loud for calls?"
- "Will it fit in my apartment?"
- "Will I actually use it after the first week?"
- "Is it safe to walk while working?"
- "Why not buy a cheaper generic walking pad?"

## Proof Points

- Fictional internal survey: 72% of beta testers used the treadmill at least 4 days per week after 30 days.
- Fictional customer review theme: users mention quietness and convenience more often than calorie burn.
- Product dimensions: 45 in x 20 in x 5 in when folded.

## Constraints

- Avoid medical claims.
- Do not imply guaranteed weight loss.
- Emphasize movement, routine, and comfort rather than disease prevention.
